# Refund Policy

## How do I request a refund?
Send your order number and a short explanation to support. Refund requests are reviewed after we verify the return status and the payment method used.

## How long do refunds take?
Approved refunds usually appear in 5 to 10 business days, depending on the bank or payment provider.

## Can shipping fees be refunded?
Shipping fees are refundable only when the refund is caused by our error, such as a wrong item or a damaged shipment.

## What if my refund is rejected?
If a refund is rejected, we will explain the reason and share the next possible step, such as a replacement or a return review.
