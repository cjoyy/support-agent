# Account Security

## How do I secure my account?
Use a unique password, avoid sharing your login details, and enable two-factor authentication if it is available in your account settings.

## How do I know if my account was accessed by someone else?
Watch for unknown orders, address changes, password reset emails, or sign-in alerts from devices you do not recognize.

## What should I do if I suspect fraud?
Change your password immediately, review recent activity, and contact support so we can review the account for suspicious behavior.

## Does support ever ask for my password?
No. Support will never ask for your password or verification codes. If someone requests them, treat it as a security risk.
