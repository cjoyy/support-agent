# Shipping Delays

## Why is my shipment delayed?
Delays can happen because of weather, customs checks, courier congestion, or an incomplete address. We monitor delayed packages and update customers by email when new tracking details are available.

## Can I change the delivery address?
Address changes are possible only before the parcel is handed to the courier. After that point, the courier may not allow rerouting.

## What should I do if my package is marked delivered but I cannot find it?
Check with neighbors, front desk staff, or household members first. If the parcel is still missing after 24 hours, contact support so we can investigate with the courier.
