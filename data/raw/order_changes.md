# Order Changes

## Can I combine two orders?
We do not merge separate orders after checkout. If you need to add items, place a new order and contact support if the first order has not shipped yet.

## Why is my order on hold?
Orders may be placed on hold when payment verification is pending, the shipping address looks incomplete, or stock needs to be confirmed. We will email you if any action is needed.

## What happens if an item is out of stock?
If one item in your order is unavailable, we may ship the remaining items first and send the missing item later, or we may contact you to approve a substitution or refund.
