# Shipping FAQ

## How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping usually takes 3 to 5 business days after the order leaves the warehouse. Remote areas can take longer depending on the courier.

## How do I track my package?
You can track your package using the tracking number sent by email. The tracking link is also available in your account order history.

## What if the tracking number does not work?
Tracking numbers may take up to 24 hours to become active after the label is created. If it still does not work after that, contact support with your order number.

## Do you ship on weekends?
Warehouse processing happens on business days only, so orders placed on weekends are usually processed on the next working day.
