# Order FAQ

## How do I place an order?
Choose the product, add it to your cart, and complete checkout with your shipping details and payment method. You will receive an order confirmation by email after payment succeeds.

## Can I change my order after payment?
If the order has not entered fulfillment, contact support as soon as possible. We can usually update the shipping address or correct a typo before the package is packed.

## How do I check my order status?
Use the order tracking link in your confirmation email or log in to your account and open the Orders page.

## Can I cancel an order?
Orders can be canceled only before they are packed or handed to the courier. Once shipping starts, the order must follow the return process.
