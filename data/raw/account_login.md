# Account Login

## How do I reset my password?
Click Forgot Password on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the reset link we send. The link expires after a limited time for security.

## Why can't I log in?
Common reasons include a wrong password, an unverified email address, or a temporary account lock after several failed attempts.

## Can I change my email address?
Yes. Open account settings and update your email, then confirm the change through the verification message we send to the new address.

## What if I do not receive the reset email?
Check your spam folder and confirm that the email address matches your account. If it still does not arrive, ask support to resend the link.
